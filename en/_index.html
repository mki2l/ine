About Ine

The Charm of Ine

Located at the eastern tip of the Tango Peninsula in northern Kyoto,
Funaya are traditional structures with boat garages on the first floor
and living quarters above - a unique architectural style of this region.

Important Preservation District

Designated as an Important Preservation District for Groups of Traditional Buildings in 2005. The fishing village life from the Edo period continues to this day.

Four Seasons Beauty

Cherry blossoms in spring, blue seas in summer, autumn foliage, and snowy winter landscapes. Enjoy the funaya scenery unique to each season.

Local Guide Tours

Guides born and raised here share the charms of Ine that you will not find in any guidebook.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation?

Yes, advance reservations are recommended. Weekends, holidays, Golden Week, and Obon periods tend to be especially busy, so we recommend booking early. Same-day reservations may be possible if space is available, but to ensure your spot, please book at least one day in advance.

Are tours held in rainy weather?

Tours operate in light rain. For the Sea Taxi Plan, we have boats with covered areas for your comfort. However, tours may be cancelled for safety reasons in case of strong winds or severe weather. We will contact you the day before or on the morning of the tour if cancellation is necessary.

Can children participate?

Absolutely! Families with children are welcome. Children under elementary school age must be accompanied by a guardian. Life jackets are provided on the Sea Taxi for your safety. Children under 3 years old are free of charge.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a free parking lot (approximately 30 spaces) located near the meeting point. If our lot is full, we can direct you to nearby paid parking options.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made by the day before the tour are free of charge. Same-day cancellations incur a 50% fee, and no-shows are charged the full amount. There is no cancellation fee if the tour is cancelled due to weather conditions.

Are English-speaking guides available?

Yes, we have English-speaking guides available. Please indicate your preference for an English guide when making your reservation. We also have English information materials to help you enjoy your experience.
