About Ine

The Charm of Ine

Weave your own story of Ine.
A private, custom-made Funaya guide exclusive to one group per day.

Our guide does not just give a one-sided explanation.
We deeply care about "what kind of time you want to spend here."
For those who wish to savor the scenery in tranquility, those who love photography,
or those who want to hear about local life and hidden history.
We uncover the deep-rooted "reasons" behind the unique landscape of Ine—something you can never see just by looking at a "beautiful view"—as we walk together.

"It feels like an immersive, deep-dive documentary!"
This is a guide designed for you to truly "experience" Ine.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a reservation required?

Yes, advance reservations are highly recommended. Weekends, national holidays, Golden Week, and the Obon period can get very crowded, so early booking is advised. Same-day bookings are accepted if slots are available, but to guarantee your tour, please book at least one day in advance.

Will the tour operate in rainy weather?

Tours will run in light rain. The Sea Taxi plan utilizes covered boats, so you can enjoy the tour comfortably. However, tours may be canceled in the event of strong winds or severe weather for safety. In such cases, we will contact you the day before or on the morning of the tour.

Can children participate?

Absolutely! Families with children are more than welcome. Children of elementary school age and younger must be accompanied by a guardian. Life jackets are fully equipped on the Sea Taxi for your peace of mind. Children aged 3 and under can join for free.

Is parking available?

Yes, we provide free parking spaces (accommodating up to approximately 30 vehicles) right next to the meeting location for those arriving by car. If the lot is full, we will direct you to nearby paid parking areas.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellations made up to the day before the tour are free of charge. Same-day cancellations will incur a 50% fee, and no-shows will be charged 100%. No cancellation fees apply if the tour is canceled due to bad weather.

Is English assistance available?

Yes, we have English-speaking guides. Please request an English guide when making your reservation. We also provide English explanatory materials so that our international guests can fully enjoy the experience.
